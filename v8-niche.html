Treehouse Boutique Hotel

A small adults-only hotel tucked among the trees in the Santa Ana neighbourhood of Mérida. Fifteen rooms, a pool, and the kind of quiet that is hard to find in the middle of a city. It became the first hotel in Mérida to receive a Michelin Key, something we are genuinely proud of.

Boutique byThe Museo

A family-friendly boutique hotel in the historic centre of Mérida, right across from the Palacio Cantón. A colonial-style escape in the heart of the city.

Pan & Kof.feé

Our artisanal espresso bar and bakery. Everything is baked from scratch every morning, the bread is sourdough, the pastries are laminated by hand, and the menu changes with what is in season. The space has two floors, a glass kitchen you can watch from your table, and enough room to sit and stay a while.

Pan & Kof.feé Roastery

A pursuit of the finest coffee Mexico has to offer. Single-origin beans from Veracruz, Chiapas and Oaxaca, roasted in small batches in Mérida. Available through the collection and in the United States.
